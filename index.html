Agriturismo Villa Rasenna

Go Green and Experience a Comfortable Organic Tuscan Farm with Private Pool

Enjoy a Tuscan experience on our organic farm, enchanting and cosy, it is nestled in the olive groves on a south-facing hillside, on the edge of the Apennines. During warm weather, stroll outside in the garden to sip your morning cappuccino and enjoy an amazing view of the lush Tuscan landscape. Sweet fragrant lavender, rosemary and thyme fill the air, find a comfy corner to sit and absorb the beauty around you and the sounds of the church bells pealing in the distance. In the summer months soak up the sun or enjoy the large pool. The facade of this three-story ancient watermill is built from the local sandstone, ‘Pietra Serena’ with it's soft ochres and greys.

ABOUT

Lovingly kept, the villa retains its rustic charm. Your own private ground floor entrance leads into the two-story home. Try our prize-winning extra virgin organic olive oil made from our own hand picked olives.

The rental property is spread out on 2 floors. Three bedrooms and a bathroom upstairs and the kitchen, sitting room, master bedroom and bathroom on the lower floor. The semi basement floor is not included in the rental.

We have tried to make our villa comfortable and cosy so our guests can feel completely at home. The villa is surrounded by fields, but only a short walk from our very friendly bar where a classic italian breakfast or aperitivo at sunset is served with a smile. We are always on call for any questions or help planning your stay with us. Cortona has been a lifelong experience for us. Slip into our world of amazing wines, exquisite food and the italian lifestyle.

Our working organic farm is famous for it's olive oil. It is a model of healthy, sustainable living set in the Tuscan hills. Extra virgin olive oil is produced from hand picked olives that are crushed in a stone ground cold pressed mill and can be purchased directly from the farm. The large swimming pool (33 x 24ft) is located on a terrace above the house, with a surround carved out of local stone, to minimize the impact on the environment, and offers a natural inviting retreat during the long hot summer days. We have an incredible olive wood gazebo next to the pool. The garden areas almost encircle the house and there are many magical settings where you can enjoy a drink or a meal outside. There are numerous exciting walks up into the mountains or down into valley. Once part of the ancient kingdom of Etruria, the hills hold many secrets; legend has it that the Greek hero Ulysses, is buried in a nearby field.
